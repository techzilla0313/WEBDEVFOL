Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a focus on Web Development is not my first choice, it is Civil Engineering, but I got hooked with it since the pandemic started that's why I changed my course and study it instead. I find it enjoyable and challenging because whenever I'm doing coding, sometimes I'm running out of ideas and I don't know how to do a certain task. But once I already know how to do it, it is really fulfilling and the joy that I'm experiencing is really worth it. Some language is easy to understand because some language is similar to the previous language that I learned. Some are hard because I donn't have the slightest idea on how to do it.


Looking ahead, I'm enthusiastic about the endless possibilities in the field of Information Technology. As I progress through my studies, I'm eager to combine my academic foundation with practical experiences, contributing to the dynamic landscape of Web Development.
